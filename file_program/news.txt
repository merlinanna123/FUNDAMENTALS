Wayanad, a hilly district in the Western Ghats mountain range, 
is prone to landslides during the monsoon season.
Due to heavy rainfall in the area,
the residents in the Punchrimattom,
Attamala and Mundakkai areas were shifted by the local body authorities to relief camps on 29 June.
Despite an alert issued by the district administration, 
many residents of Chooramala area stayed in their houses as it was not marked as a landslide-prone area.
